SQL

Over the summer of 2023, I had the enriching opportunity to work with Lake Forest College, where I delved into the realm of SQL Server Management Studio. My primary focus was on crafting tailored queries to generate athlete data for the Athletics Department. This hands-on experience not only deepened my understanding of database management but also honed my analytical skills as I uncovered insights crucial for decision-making in creating complex queries. Additionally, I gained valuable exposure to Jenzabar, a robust student information system widely used in higher education.

During this semester, I collaborated with my research partner to integrate their AI model into Jenzabar using Constituo at Lake Forest College. This involved navigating the intricacies of both the AI model and the college's information system to ensure seamless integration. It was a challenging yet rewarding experience, as we worked to leverage a complex technology to enhance the capabilities of the existing infrastructure. This project not only expanded my technical skills but also provided valuable insights into the practical applications of AI in educational settings.

Classes taken at LFC:
CS 488 - Senior Seminar (API and Database work)
